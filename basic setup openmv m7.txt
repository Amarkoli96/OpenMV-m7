1 ] Download the IDE from "https://openmv.io/pages/download" and install as per your operating system
2 ] Open IDE after installation
3 ] Connect USB to COM Port
	-first it will ask for firmware update, update it(for IDE)
	-then it will ask for the previous program to erase and re-programming
	-next it will ask which device we are connecting m7/h7 etc
4 ] Click on File->Examples select basic examples
5 ] Click on the connect button and your device is connected 
6 ] click no |> play button to run script to the Openmv Camera
